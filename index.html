Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You will win by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game is your total score goes above the random number.
The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.
Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of the crystals each time.
